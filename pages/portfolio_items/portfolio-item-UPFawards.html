UPF awards: Best high school research project

ABOUT THE AWARDS

In Catalonia 10% of the final grade of the high school is based on a final research project. Some students decide to present their project to different universities awards. The Pompeu Fabra university (UPF) awards the best project about mathematics and engineering. It selects 10 of the submitted projects to make a presentation to the judges and also some UPF student make a presentation about his final thesis.

ABOUT MY PRESENTATION

The university asked me to talk about my final thesis and artificial intelligence in general. It was a first approach to the presentation of my final thesis I am doing on June. I start presenting different areas inside artificial intelligence with some examples. Then I talk about how reinforcement learning works in a very general way. And finally what improvements I have made in the state of the art algorithms.
The presentation is in Spanish.

Artificial Intelligence and Deep Reinforcement Learning de Jorge Aleman Gonzalez
